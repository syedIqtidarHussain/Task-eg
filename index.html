RED CARD 2018

China Digital Football Awards

The Red Card is globally recognized as the leading annual report to assess the most influential European Football Clubs, Leagues, and Players online in China. Now in its 7th year, Red Card 2018 has been expanded to include 5 additional award categories to recognize the many areas of digital performance in China.

It is our aim to educate fans and organizations on how to improve their China digital strategy, host today's Chinese fan base, and see what works online in China.
